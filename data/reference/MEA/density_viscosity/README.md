# MEA Density and Viscosity Extraction Targets

This folder is reserved for machine-readable density and viscosity data extracted from repo-local literature Markdown.

Initial source:

- `docs/papers/md/Amundsen et al. - 2009 - Density and viscosity of monoethanolamine + water + carbon dioxide from (25 to 80) °C.md`

Required columns are defined in `data/reference/MEA/manifests/source_status_manifest.csv` and `docs/roadmaps/mea_data_curation_plan.md`.

Do not add estimated or fabricated rows.
